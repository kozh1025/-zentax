Q1
The HR department will ＿＿ each employee's performance at the end of the fiscal year to determine eligibility for a salary increase.
【中譯】人力資源部門將在財政年度結束時__每位員工的績效，以決定薪資調漲的資格。
A
appraise
B
review
C
evaluate
D
assess
解題關鍵
evaluate 最適合描述對員工整體績效進行系統性評估的動作，常與 performance、effectiveness 搭配，語境為正式人事考核流程。
陷阱提示
appraise 多用於資產鑑價；review 偏向「審閱文件或過程」；assess 在財務或學術測驗語境中更自然，而非人事評核的核心用字。

evaluatev./ɪˈvæljueɪt/評估（員工整體績效或系統成效）The HR department will evaluate each employee's performance at the end of the fiscal year to determine eligibility for a salary increase. ✓
assessv./əˈses/評定（通常用於考試成績或財務稅務）The tax office assessed the total income of the company for last year.

Q2
＿＿ did the proposed changes reduce operating costs, but they also improved overall team morale.
【中譯】所提出的變更 __ 降低了營運成本，還改善了整體團隊士氣。
A
Not only
B
Only not
C
Not only they
D
They not only
解題關鍵
否定副詞片語 Not only 置於句首時，主句必須倒裝（助動詞提前）：Not only + did + S + V...，but also 引導第二子句恢復正常語序。
陷阱提示
(C) Not only they 是最常見陷阱：學習者知道用 Not only 但忘記倒裝，保留了正常語序 they + did，導致語法錯誤。
文法補充
否定倒裝：Not only + aux./do + S + V..., but also... 類似結構：Neither/Nor did S V；Seldom/Rarely/Never + aux. + S + V
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
Not only否定副詞片語（倒裝首）置首觸發主句倒裝：Not only + did + S + VNot only...but also 標準倒裝結構 ✓
Only not錯誤語序非標準英語結構，語意與語序均錯誤✗ 不存在此結構
Not only they缺倒裝（保留正常語序）否定副詞置首但未倒裝，語法錯誤✗ 須倒裝：Not only did they
They not only無倒裝（主語前置）主語 They 在前，not only 置中，無倒裝效果✗ 非強調倒裝結構

【中譯】這項憲法修正案旨在__監管機構的權力，要求所有重大規則變更在生效前均須獲得立法機關批准。

D

amplifyv/ˈæmplɪfaɪ/擴大；增強amplify the signal（擴大，語意相反）
articulatev/ɑːrˈtɪkjuleɪt/清楚表達；闡述articulate a position（表達立場，語意不符）

Q6
Despite the increased workload following the merger, the customer service team remained ＿＿_____ and continued to resolve complaints efficiently.
【中譯】儘管合併後工作量增加，客服團隊依然______，並持續有效地處理客訴。
A
productively
B
productivity
C
productive
D
production
解題關鍵
remain 是連綴動詞（linking verb），其後須接形容詞作主詞補語，描述主詞的狀態。productive（形容詞）正確填入，表示「團隊保持有效率的狀態」。
陷阱提示
productively 是副詞，副詞不能作連綴動詞的主詞補語（不能說 remain productively）；productivity 與 production 均為名詞，語法上雖可接在動詞後，但語意不通順，且 remain 後接名詞需邏輯對等（如 remain a leader）。
文法補充
連綴動詞（linking verbs）包括 be、remain、become、seem、appear、feel、look、sound、taste、smell 等，其後接形容詞（非副詞）作主詞補語（subject complement）。常見錯誤：誤用副詞（-ly）代替形容詞。口訣：連綴動詞 = 等號，形容詞描述主詞狀態。
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
productivelyadv./prəˈdʌktɪvlɪ/有成效地（副詞，不能接在連綴動詞後）work productively
productivityn./ˌprɒdʌkˈtɪvɪtɪ/生產力（名詞，文法上不符合此位置）increase productivity
productiveadj./prəˈdʌktɪv/有生產力的；有成效的（形容詞，連綴動詞補語）remain productive / stay productive
productionn./prəˈdʌkʃən/生產；製作（名詞，語意與語境不符）mass production / film production
Q7

untilprep/conj/ənˈtɪl/直到…（終點截止）valid until + 截止日 ✓

Q13
Under no circumstances ＿＿ the researchers permitted to share identifiable participant data with any third parties outside the approved clinical research network.
【中譯】在任何情況下，研究人員均__被允許將可辨識的受試者資料分享給核准臨床研究網絡以外的任何第三方。
A
are
B
were
C
the researchers are
D
they are
解題關鍵
Under no circumstances + aux. + S + V = 否定狀語前置倒裝：否定副詞片語置於句首時，助動詞必須移至主語之前；此為正式書面英語規定語氣
陷阱提示
正常語序（the researchers are / they are）不符倒裝規則；were 為過去式，此處現行政策應用現在式 are
文法補充
否定狀語前置倒裝（Negative Adverbial Inversion）：Under no circumstances / At no time / In no way / On no account + aux. + S + V（助動詞前置）。辨析：① 此結構為 C1 高級書面文體；② 後接被動語態時：Under no circumstances are + S + p.p.；③ 與 only when/not until 倒裝類型（only + adv. + aux. + S）性質相同但觸發詞不同
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
areaux./ɑːr/（助動詞，現在式）Under no circumstances + are + S + p.p. = 否定狀語前置倒裝：助動詞提前至主語之前 ✓（現在式政策聲明）
wereaux./wɜːr/（助動詞，過去式）Under no circumstances + were + S（過去式倒裝）：語態時式不符——此為現行政策規定，應用現在式 are
the researchers arephr.（主語＋助動詞，正常語序）Under no circumstances + the researchers are（✗）：否定狀語前置（Under no circumstances）後，必須倒裝，助動詞置於主語之前
they arephr.（代詞主語＋助動詞，正常語序）Under no circumstances + they are（✗）：與上項同理，否定狀語前置後不可維持正常主語＋助動詞語序

TOEIC · article
As environmental concerns move to the forefront of corporate strategy, multinational companies are under growing scrutiny from investors, regulators, and consumers alike. Increasingly, these organizations are expected not only to meet internal environmental targets but also to ensure that their suppliers adhere to comparable standards. This shift has given rise to a new discipline within procurement: sustainable supply chain management.

Leading companies in sectors ranging from consumer electronics to fast fashion have begun embedding rigorous environmental criteria into their supplier selection processes. Rather than treating sustainability as a standalone initiative, forward-thinking procurement teams now evaluate vendors on carbon emissions, water usage, waste management practices, and adherence to internationally recognized frameworks such as ISO 14001 or the UN Global Compact. Suppliers that fail to meet these benchmarks risk being dropped from approved vendor lists entirely.

The business case for such measures is compelling. Companies that align their sourcing decisions with environmental principles often find that cost savings and brand equity improvements translate into measurable financial gains over the medium to long term. Reduced energy consumption across the supply chain lowers operational costs, while demonstrated commitment to environmental stewardship attracts sustainability-conscious customers and institutional investors.

Of course, implementation is not without challenges. Smaller suppliers in emerging markets may lack the resources or technical expertise to comply with stringent environmental requirements imposed by their larger partners. To address this gap, several multinational firms have launched capacity-building programs, offering training, auditing support, and financing options to help smaller vendors meet the required thresholds. Such collaborative approaches reflect an understanding that sustainable supply chains are built through partnership, not simply through mandates.
