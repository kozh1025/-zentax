Q1
Following the routine ＿＿ of the cold storage facilities, the quality assurance team confirmed that all temperature logs were within acceptable ranges.
【中譯】在對冷藏設施進行例行__之後，品保團隊確認所有溫度記錄均在可接受範圍內。
A
inspection
B
inspect
C
inspector
D
inspective
解題關鍵
空格前有 the routine，空格後接 of the cold storage facilities → the routine __ of = 「例行的…（of + 受檢對象）」→ 需名詞 inspection；inspector 為名詞但指「人」不指「行為」
陷阱提示
inspect（動詞）vs inspection（名詞—行為）vs inspector（名詞—人）三者字形相近；本題需「行為/過程」名詞
文法補充
the + adj. + 過程名詞 + of + N 結構：the routine inspection of / the thorough evaluation of / the annual review of（均為「名詞 + of + 受檢對象」）

inspectv/ɪnˈspekt/檢查；視察（動詞）inspect facilities（動詞；不能接在 the routine 後作名詞）
inspectorn/ɪnˈspektər/檢查員；視察官（名詞—指人）the inspector（指「人」；inspection of facilities 結構下需要「行為/過程」名詞，非「人」）

Q2
Despite the increased workload following the merger, the customer service team remained ＿＿_____ and continued to resolve complaints efficiently.
【中譯】儘管合併後工作量增加，客服團隊依然______，並持續有效地處理客訴。
A
productively
B
productivity
C
productive
D
production
解題關鍵
remain 是連綴動詞（linking verb），其後須接形容詞作主詞補語，描述主詞的狀態。productive（形容詞）正確填入，表示「團隊保持有效率的狀態」。
陷阱提示
productively 是副詞，副詞不能作連綴動詞的主詞補語（不能說 remain productively）；productivity 與 production 均為名詞，語法上雖可接在動詞後，但語意不通順，且 remain 後接名詞需邏輯對等（如 remain a leader）。
文法補充
連綴動詞（linking verbs）包括 be、remain、become、seem、appear、feel、look、sound、taste、smell 等，其後接形容詞（非副詞）作主詞補語（subject complement）。常見錯誤：誤用副詞（-ly）代替形容詞。口訣：連綴動詞 = 等號，形容詞描述主詞狀態。
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
productivelyadv./prəˈdʌktɪvlɪ/有成效地（副詞，不能接在連綴動詞後）work productively
productivityn./ˌprɒdʌkˈtɪvɪtɪ/生產力（名詞，文法上不符合此位置）increase productivity
productiveadj./prəˈdʌktɪv/有生產力的；有成效的（形容詞，連綴動詞補語）remain productive / stay productive
productionn./prəˈdʌkʃən/生產；製作（名詞，語意與語境不符）mass production / film production
Q3
By the time the new wing ＿＿ completed, the hotel will have accommodated over fifty thousand guests since its founding.
【中譯】等到新翼大樓__完工時，這間飯店自創立以來將已接待超過五萬名住客。
A
will complete
B
has been completed
C
will be completing
D
is completed
解題關鍵
by the time 引導的時間副詞子句，動詞用現在式代替未來式；主詞 wing 為被受詞，需用被動 is completed。
陷阱提示
易誤選 will be completed（未來被動），但時間子句中不得使用 will。
文法補充
By the time + S + V（現在式/現在完成式），主句常搭配未來完成式 will have + p.p.。子句中 will 被時間連接詞 by the time 取代，改用現在式表未來。
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
will completev.—主動未來式主詞 the new wing 為被動，且 by the time 子句用現在式代替未來
has been completedv.—現在完成被動主句已有 will have accommodated，時態搭配不一致
will be completingv.—未來進行主動the wing 非施事者，語態錯誤
is completedv.—現在簡單被動 ✓by the time + 現在式（代未來）+ 被動語態；主句用未來完成式

Q5
The engineering team determined that the remaining server bandwidth was ＿＿ across three concurrent projects without any significant performance degradation.
【中譯】工程團隊確定，剩餘的伺服器頻寬在不造成任何顯著效能下降的情況下，是可以在三個同時進行的專案之間__的。
A
allocate
B
allocable
C
allocation
D
allocating
解題關鍵
was + 形容詞 = 連繫動詞結構，描述「頻寬的屬性/狀態」；allocable（形容詞）= 可分配的；allocation 名詞不能作形容詞補語；allocating 過去進行式語意不符。
陷阱提示
allocation（名詞）是最常見的干擾項，但名詞不能作連繫動詞後的形容詞補語；allocable 形容詞是正確答案，但較罕見，需靠文法結構判斷。
文法補充
Word Form 解題步驟：① was + __ → 連繫動詞後需形容詞（主語補語）② 四選項：動詞/形容詞/名詞/分詞 → 選形容詞 allocable ③ allocable = 可分配的（技術/財務文件常用形容詞：allocable costs/bandwidth/resources）
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
allocatev/ˈæləkeɪt/分配；撥出（動詞原形）was allocate（was + 動詞原形，語法錯誤；was 後需接形容詞或過去分詞）
allocableadj/ˈæləkəbl/可分配的；可撥用的（形容詞）was allocable ✓（was + 形容詞；allocable = 可被分配的，技術文件常用形容詞；整句：頻寬是可被分配到三個專案的）
allocationn/ˌæləˈkeɪʃən/分配；撥款（名詞）was allocation（was + 名詞不作形容詞補語；需要形容詞）
allocatingv/ˈæləkeɪtɪŋ/正在分配（現在分詞/動名詞）was allocating（be + V-ing = 過去進行式；語意不符，此句非描述進行中的動作而是確認頻寬的屬性）

解題關鍵

Q9
The compliance officer reminded all staff to double-check their documentation, ＿＿ any discrepancies __ go undetected before the external audit.
【中譯】合規主任提醒所有員工仔細核查文件，以免任何差異在外部稽核前未被察覺。
A
so that any discrepancies might go undetected
B
in order that any discrepancies could go undetected
C
lest any discrepancies should go undetected
D
unless any discrepancies were to go undetected
解題關鍵
lest + S + should + V 為否定目的子句，表「以免……」，語氣正式，常見於商業、法律文件。so that / in order that 後接的子句表「為了……」（正面目的），語意相反。unless 表「除非」，語意不符。lest 後的子句不可加 not，should 為語氣助動詞。
陷阱提示
so that / lest 均為目的子句連接詞，但 so that 為正面目的（為了達成），lest 為負面目的（以免發生）。考生易混淆兩者方向性。
文法補充
lest + S + should + V 否定目的子句（C1 正式文法）：lest 本身含否定，後不加 not；should 為語氣助動詞，可省略但書面語保留
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
so that any discrepancies might go undetected
in order that any discrepancies could go undetected
lest any discrepancies should go undetected
unless any discrepancies were to go undetected
字詞家族
lest
conj. 以免、唯恐（正式）
for fear that
conj. 唯恐（同義，口語稍多）
in case
conj. 以防（預防性動作，語意略有別）
so that
conj. 以便（正面目的，方向相反）
Q10
During the presentation, the speaker ＿＿ answer a question from the audience before continuing with the next slide.
【中譯】在簡報過程中，講者 __ 回答了一位聽眾的提問，然後才繼續下一張投影片。
A
stopped answering
B
stopped answer
C
stopped to answer
D
had stopped answering
解題關鍵
stop to answer 表示「停下（簡報）去做另一件事（回答問題）」，符合語境：講者中斷簡報進行，暫停下來回答聽眾提問後再繼續。
陷阱提示
stop answering 表示「永久停止回答」，如 He stopped answering calls（他不再接電話了），與本題語意相反；had stopped answering 過去完成式與句中時態不符。
文法補充
stop + V-ing：停止（正在做）的事，動作終止。例：She stopped talking（她停止說話）。stop + to V：停下來去做另一件事，動作轉換。例：She stopped to talk（她停下來去說話）。兩者意義截然不同，需依語境判斷。
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法

stopped answerv. phrase (incorrect)/stɒpt ˈɑːnsər/（語法錯誤形式）stop 後不可直接接原形動詞
stopped to answerv. phrase (infinitive)/stɒpt tə ˈɑːnsər/停下來（去）回答（暫停原動作以做另一件事）stop + to V = 停下手邊的事，去做另一件事

Lessons from National Immunization Campaigns: What Works and Why

Public health authorities around the world have long recognized that producing a safe and effective vaccine is only half the challenge. The other half — convincing populations to accept it — requires a carefully calibrated communication and logistics strategy that takes into account local cultural, economic, and informational contexts.

Successful immunization campaigns share several observable characteristics. First and foremost, they prioritize trust-building over information delivery. Studies consistently show that communities are more likely to accept vaccines when guidance comes from trusted local figures — community leaders, religious authorities, and neighborhood healthcare workers — rather than from centralized government bodies alone. This does not diminish the role of national health agencies, but it does suggest that top-down communication alone is insufficient.

Accessibility is the second critical factor. Campaigns that remove logistical barriers — by bringing vaccines directly to workplaces, schools, and community centers, offering extended hours, and eliminating out-of-pocket costs — consistently achieve higher uptake rates than those relying solely on fixed clinic appointments. Mobile vaccination units have proven particularly effective in reaching rural and underserved populations.

Finally, effective campaigns address vaccine hesitancy proactively rather than reactively. Waiting until misinformation has taken hold before launching corrections tends to be far less effective than preemptively equipping communities with accurate, accessible information. Notwithstanding the inherent challenges of combating false narratives online, health authorities that invest early in community engagement programs have consistently reported stronger vaccination coverage across demographic groups.

The evidence points to a clear conclusion: immunization success is not primarily a scientific problem but a social one.
