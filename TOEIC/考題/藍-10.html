Q1
The project director had the legal team ------- all vendor contracts before the partnership agreement was signed.
【中譯】專案總監讓法務團隊在合夥協議簽署前審查了所有供應商合約。
A
to review
B
reviewing
C
reviewed
D
review
解題關鍵
使役動詞 have 的結構：have + 受詞（人）+ 原形動詞（V bare）。本句受詞為 the legal team（人），其後需接原形動詞 review，表示「讓法務團隊審查」。to review 為不定詞，用於 ask/want/expect 等非使役動詞；reviewing 為進行分詞，不用於此結構；reviewed 為過去分詞，用於 have + 物 + pp 的被動使役結構（如 had the contracts reviewed）。
陷阱提示
reviewed 容易混淆：had + 物（contracts）+ pp 表被動使役（合約被審查）；但本句受詞為人（the legal team），應接原形動詞 review，兩種結構需分辨清楚。
文法補充
使役動詞 have 的兩種結構：① have + 人 + V（原形）→ 主動，表示讓某人做某事；② have + 物 + pp（過去分詞）→ 被動，表示讓某物被處理。
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
to review不定詞/tuː rɪˈvjuː/審查（to V）asked someone to review
reviewing現在分詞/rɪˈvjuːɪŋ/審查（進行中）is reviewing
reviewed過去分詞/rɪˈvjuːd/被審查（被動）had contracts reviewed
review原形動詞/rɪˈvjuː/審查（原形）had the team review

Q6
＿＿ proposals employees submit through the internal feedback system, the management team reviews them within five business days.
【中譯】__員工透過內部回饋系統提交的建議，管理團隊都會在五個工作日內進行審核。
A
Whatever
B
Whenever
C
However
D
Wherever
解題關鍵
Whatever proposals = no matter what proposals，為複合關係代名詞引導的讓步子句，後可直接接名詞作受詞；Whenever 表時間、However 後接形容詞或副詞、Wherever 表地點，三者均不能直接接名詞。
陷阱提示
However 最易被誤選，但 However + adj./adv.（如 However difficult）結構後不接名詞
文法補充
Whatever 作複合關係代名詞時，結構為 Whatever + N + S + V，相當於 No matter what + N + S + V。例：Whatever decision you make = No matter what decision you make（無論你做什麼決定）。
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
Whatever複合關係代名詞/wɒtˈevər/無論什麼（+ 名詞）Whatever + N + S + V（讓步子句；= no matter what + N）✓
Whenever複合關係副詞/wenˈevər/無論何時Whenever + S + V（表時間讓步，不接名詞）
However複合關係副詞/haʊˈevər/無論如何；程度讓步However + adj./adv. + S + V（不接名詞）
Wherever複合關係副詞/werˈevər/無論何地Wherever + S + V（表地點讓步，不接名詞）

The Rise of Flexible Workspaces: How Hybrid Work Is Reshaping Commercial Real Estate

The widespread adoption of hybrid work models since 2020 has fundamentally altered the demand dynamics of commercial office real estate. According to a 2025 report by the Urban Property Research Council (UPRC), the average office occupancy rate in major metropolitan areas has stabilized at approximately 58% of pre-pandemic levels, a figure that has prompted many landlords and property developers to fundamentally reconsider their offerings.

These converted spaces command a premium of 15% to 30% over standard office rates, according to the UPRC findings, as tenants are willing to pay for built-in flexibility and reduced fit-out costs.

Analysts project that this trend will accelerate over the next five years, with demand for fully fitted, flexible office space expected to outpace supply in key urban markets by 2028.
