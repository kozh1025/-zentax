Q1
The board has approved the ＿＿ of the headquarters to a new business park on the outskirts of the city, effective from the first quarter of next year.
【中譯】董事會已批准總部__至城市郊區一處新商業園區，自明年第一季起生效。
A
locate
B
location
C
locating
D
relocation
解題關鍵
空格前 The（定冠詞）+ 空格 + of the headquarters → 需名詞作主語 → relocation（含搬遷動作義）✓；location 語意偏靜態位置；locate 是動詞；locating 動名詞搭配不自然
陷阱提示
location 形狀最相近但語意為「靜態地點」而非「搬遷行為」；relocation 強調「重新安置」的動作
文法補充
Word Form 解題步驟：① 空格前 The（定冠詞）後接名詞 ② approved the __ of... = 批准某動作 → 需動作名詞 → relocation（搬遷）✓ ③ location 為靜態位置名詞；relocation 為動作名詞（re- 前綴 = 重新）
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
locatev/loʊˈkeɪt/定位；確定地點（動詞原形）locate a branch（動詞；定冠詞 The 後不接動詞原形）
locationn/loʊˈkeɪʃən/地點；位置（名詞）The location is convenient（名詞；但語意偏「現有位置」不含「搬遷」的動作義）
locatingv/loʊˈkeɪtɪŋ/定位中（現在分詞/動名詞）locating a site（動名詞；定冠詞 The + 動名詞語法上罕見且語意不符「搬遷批准」）
relocationn/ˌriːloʊˈkeɪʃən/遷移；搬遷（名詞）the relocation of the headquarters ✓（定冠詞 + 名詞 + of = 主語位置；含「重新安置」動作義）
字詞家族
locate
v. 找到…的位置；使…坐落於
location
n. 地點；位置
relocation
n. 遷移；重新安置
relocate
v. 搬遷；重新安置

A

B

C

D

Q3
Residents are reminded that entry to the restricted evacuation zones during the drill requires written ＿＿ from the City Emergency Management Office.
【中譯】居民須知：在演習期間進入限制撤離區域，需要市緊急事務管理辦公室的書面 __。
A
permit
B
permission
C
permissive
D
permitting
解題關鍵
written __ from the Office → 需名詞；written permission（書面許可/授權）為固定搭配。
陷阱提示
permit 也可作名詞（許可證），但 written permission 為行政授權的固定用語更正式；permissive/permitting 不符名詞位置。
文法補充
Word Form 解題步驟：1. written __ → 需名詞；2. permit 字根：permit(v/n)/permission(n)/permissive(adj)/permitting(v-ing)；3. written permission 為固定搭配。
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
permitv/n/ˈpɜːrmɪt/允許；許可（名詞為許可証）permit 作動詞，requires + 名詞 位置需名詞（雖 permit 也可作名詞，但 permission 更正式指許可授權）
permissionn/pərˈmɪʃən/許可；批准；授權written permission from…（書面授權）—正式行政用語
permissiveadj/pərˈmɪsɪv/寬容的；許可的permissive 為形容詞，不符名詞位置
permittingv-ing/pərˈmɪtɪŋ/允許中；（天氣）若允許permitting 作動名詞或插入語，不符 written __ 結構
字詞家族

v./n. 允許；許可；許可證
permission
n. 許可；授權；批准
permissive
adj. 寬容的；過度縱容的
permitting
v-ing. 允許（進行式）；（weather permitting 天氣允許的話）

Q5
Critics argued that the regulatory body's failure to act against known violations reflected a ＿＿ approach that undermined public trust in the oversight system.
【中譯】批評者認為，監管機構對已知違規行為未採取行動，反映出一種__的態度，損害了公眾對監督體系的信任。
A
cautious
B
indifferent
C
conservative
D
pusillanimous
解題關鍵
pusillanimous = 膽小怯懦而不採取行動（C1批評書面語）；與「failure to act」完美呼應；cautious/conservative 為中性；indifferent 偏漠然
陷阱提示
cautious 與 pusillanimous 均與「不積極行動」有關，但 pusillanimous 帶有強烈批評語氣（懦弱），cautious 為中性謹慎
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
cautiousadj/ˈkɔːʃəs/謹慎的（中性偏正面）a cautious approach（偏謹慎小心，不含批評語氣；不如 pusillanimous 精確）
indifferentadj/ɪnˈdɪfərənt/漠不關心的（中性語氣）an indifferent attitude（偏漠然，不強調「因膽怯而不作為」）
conservativeadj/kənˈsɜːrvətɪv/保守的（中性）a conservative approach（偏保守觀念，不含批評語氣）
pusillanimousadj/ˌpjuːsɪˈlænɪməs/膽小怯懦的；懦弱的（C1批評語氣）a pusillanimous approach ✓（C1正式批評用語，指因膽怯而不敢採取行動；與「failure to act」批評語境完美呼應）
字詞家族
pusillanimous
adj. 膽小怯懦的；懦弱的

n. 膽怯；懦弱

adj. 懦弱的（相近，較口語）

adj. 膽怯的（相近，B1）

TOEIC · article
Blockchain technology, long associated with cryptocurrency, is now finding practical application in the food service industry as a tool for supply chain transparency. Several restaurant chains and food distributors have begun piloting systems that record each step of a product's journey — from farm to processing facility to distribution hub — on a shared digital ledger. Because entries on a blockchain cannot be altered retroactively, the technology offers a level of traceability that traditional paper-based records cannot match.

Advocates argue that this transparency directly benefits consumers, restaurant operators, and regulators alike. When a food safety incident occurs, blockchain records allow investigators to pinpoint the origin of contaminated produce within hours rather than days. Industry observers note that this capability has the potential to significantly reduce both the scope of product recalls and associated financial losses. Several pilot programs report that the average time required to trace a product back to its source has been reduced by over seventy percent compared with conventional tracking methods.

Despite these promising results, widespread adoption faces notable obstacles. The cost of integrating blockchain systems with existing supply chain infrastructure remains high, particularly for smaller suppliers and independent restaurant operators. Some critics also caution that the technology is only as reliable as the data entered at each stage; if a supplier falsifies information at the point of origin, the blockchain will faithfully record that false data. For now, the industry views blockchain as a promising complement to — rather than a complete replacement for — conventional food safety audits.
