Q1

【中譯】基金經理強調，投資組合__仍然是降低市場波動風險最有效的策略。
A
diversify
B
diversification
C
diversified
D

解題關鍵
portfolio diversification = 複合名詞主語（投資組合多元化）；remains 為述語，空格為名詞，作 portfolio 的複合主語核心
陷阱提示
diversify 動詞不能接在 portfolio 後作名詞；diversified 形容詞在此位置需冠詞（a diversified portfolio）；diversely 為副詞

Word Form 解題步驟：① portfolio __ remains the most effective strategy → portfolio + __ = 複合名詞主語，remains 為述語 ② 空格為名詞（與 portfolio 構成複合主語） ③ diversify（v）→ diversification（n）→ diversified（adj/p.p.）→ diversely（adv）→ 選名詞 diversification

The Rise of Co-Working Spaces and the Shifting Corporate Landscape

Over the past decade, co-working spaces have transformed from a niche concept favored by freelancers into a mainstream feature of the urban business landscape. Once dismissed as temporary solutions for small-scale entrepreneurs, these shared workplaces now attract established corporations, technology firms, and even government agencies seeking greater flexibility in their real estate strategies. Industry analysts estimate that the number of co-working locations globally has grown by more than 200 percent since 2015, with no signs of slowing down.

The appeal of co-working spaces extends well beyond cost savings. Companies report that employees working in shared environments demonstrate higher levels of creativity and cross-sector collaboration compared with those confined to traditional office settings. The proximity to professionals from different industries fosters an exchange of ideas that many organizations find difficult to replicate internally. Furthermore, the on-demand nature of co-working memberships allows businesses to scale their workspace needs up or down in response to project timelines and headcount fluctuations.

Despite these advantages, some executives remain cautious. Critics point out that open-plan shared environments can compromise confidentiality, particularly for firms handling sensitive legal or financial data. Others note that a transient workforce may erode the sense of shared identity and organizational loyalty that traditionally strengthens corporate culture. As businesses continue to evaluate the trade-offs, co-working is expected to remain a significant and evolving component of the modern workplace ecosystem.
