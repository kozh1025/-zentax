Q9

A

B

C

D

解題關鍵
be required to-inf 是被動式的固定結構：S + be + required + to + V。主動式 require sb to-inf，被動式 sb be required to-inf。

Q10
Many new employees find the onboarding process ------- because it involves multiple departments and requires completion of numerous forms.
【中譯】許多新進員工覺得入職流程很複雜，因為它涉及多個部門，且需要填寫大量表格。
A
complication
B
complicate
C
complicated
D
complicating
解題關鍵
find + O + adj（受詞補語）結構：find the onboarding process complicated，空格填形容詞 complicated 作為受詞補語，描述受詞的狀態，語法正確。
陷阱提示
complication 是名詞，不能作受詞補語；complicate 是動詞原形，語法不合；complicating 為現在分詞，若放此處會被誤解為分詞片語，但語意不自然且文法有歧義。
文法補充
SVOC 結構：find / keep / make / consider + 受詞 + 形容詞（受詞補語）。例：find the task difficult、keep the door open、make the report clear。
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
complicationn./ˌkɒmplɪˈkeɪʃn/複雜化；併發症cause complications

Q14
If the marketing team ＿＿ the budget proposal earlier, the campaign would have launched on schedule.
【中譯】如果行銷團隊早些__預算提案，這個活動就會按時啟動了。
A
submitted
B
had submitted
C
would submit
D
has submitted
解題關鍵
第三條件句（反事實條件）公式：If + S + had + pp（過去完成式）, S + would have + pp。本題 if 子句需用過去完成式 had submitted，表示對過去事實的假設。
文法補充
第三條件句（Unreal Past Conditional）：If + S + had + Vpp, S + would have + Vpp。用於對過去已發生事實的假設，暗示實際情況相反（行銷團隊並未提早提交）。

Rising Return Rates in E-Commerce: A Growing Cost Burden for Retailers

A recent industry report has identified rising return rates as one of the most pressing challenges facing online retailers today. According to the data compiled over the past three fiscal years, the average return rate for apparel sold through e-commerce platforms has climbed to 32 percent, compared with just 18 percent for purchases made in physical stores. Analysts attribute this disparity largely to the inability of online shoppers to assess product quality, fit, and color accuracy before completing a purchase.

The financial implications of high return rates are substantial. Retailers must absorb the costs of reverse logistics, including collection, sorting, inspection, and restocking. In cases where returned items cannot be resold at full price due to minor damage or repackaging issues, companies often resort to liquidating inventory at steep discounts. As a result of these compounding expenses, several mid-sized retailers reported operating margins shrinking by as much as six percentage points over the same period.

Industry experts suggest that addressing this challenge will require a multifaceted approach. Investments in augmented reality tools that allow customers to visualize products before purchase, along with stricter return windows and revised fee structures, are being evaluated by major platforms. However, critics caution that overly restrictive return policies risk alienating loyal customers, ultimately undermining the competitive advantage that convenience-driven online shopping provides.
