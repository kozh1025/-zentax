Q1
------- are required to keep all original receipts and submit them along with the completed Expense Reimbursement Form.
【中譯】________須保留所有原始收據，並連同填妥的費用報銷表格一併提交。
A
Employees traveling on business
B
Employees who they travel on business
C
Employees have traveled on business
D
Employees to travel on business
解題關鍵
Employees traveling on business
陷阱提示
B 在關係子句中 who 已充當主詞，再加 they 屬重複主詞錯誤；C 形成兩個動詞並列的句子卻無連接詞；D 的不定詞暗示「為了出差」或「將要出差」，語義不符「凡出差者均須」的政策規範。
文法補充
現在分詞片語（V-ing phrase）可直接後置修飾名詞，功能等同限制性關係子句（who/that + V）。例：Employees traveling on business = Employees who travel on business。此結構常見於正式商務文件與政策公告中，用以簡潔地限定所指涉的名詞群體。
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
Employees traveling on businessn. phrase/ˈɛmplɔɪiːz ˈtrævəlɪŋ ɒn ˈbɪznəs/出差的員工（現在分詞片語修飾名詞）✓ V-ing 片語後置修飾名詞，等同 who travel on business
Employees who they travel on businessn. clause/—/出差的員工（含多餘代名詞 they）關係子句已含 who 作主詞，再加 they 造成重複主詞語法錯誤
Employees have traveled on businessclause/—/員工已出差（獨立子句，動詞衝突）此形式為完整子句，與後方謂語 are required 之間缺乏連接詞，產生動詞衝突
Employees to travel on businessn. phrase/—/欲出差的員工（不定詞片語）不定詞片語修飾名詞時通常表「目的」或「尚未發生之動作」，不適合描述「正在/習慣性出差者」

Q4
＿＿_____ the new software update is installed on all workstations, employees will be required to complete a brief online training session.
【中譯】______新軟體更新安裝至所有工作站後，員工將被要求完成一次簡短的線上培訓課程。
A
By the time
B
Until
C
As soon as
D
Once
解題關鍵
once 表示「一旦某條件完成，隨即觸發後果」，強調安裝完成是後續培訓的先決條件，語意最完整。本句主句為未來式（will be required），時間子句用現在式（is installed），符合「時間副詞子句以現在式代替未來式」規則。
陷阱提示
by the time 強調「在某時間點之前已完成」，通常搭配完成式（had done），不適合此處；until 暗示「持續等待直到……」，語意不符；as soon as 雖也表「立即」，但 once 更強調「條件一旦滿足即觸發全面結果」，更符合全體工作站安裝完畢才統一要求培訓的情境。
文法補充
時間副詞子句中，不論主句時態為何（未來式、命令句等），子句一律以現在式（simple present）或現在完成式（present perfect）代替未來式。四個常考連接詞語意比較：(1) once = 條件一旦完成→結果啟動；(2) as soon as = 立即接續；(3) by the time = 截止時間點（常搭配完成式）；(4) until = 持續到某時間點（主句含否定或持續義）。
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
By the time連接詞片語/baɪ ðə taɪm/到……的時候（強調某動作在另一動作完成前已結束，常搭配完成式）By the time he arrived, we had left.
Until連接詞/ənˈtɪl/直到……為止（強調持續到某時間點，主句常含否定或持續義）Wait until the report is ready.
As soon as連接詞片語/æz suːn æz/一……就……（強調立即接續發生，時態對稱）Call me as soon as you arrive.
Once連接詞/wʌns/一旦……（強調條件完成後的結果，含「全部完成後才觸發」之義）Once the update is installed, restart the system.

Q12
According to the updated store policy, ＿＿ weekend shift supervisors __ part-time floor staff are authorized to approve refunds exceeding fifty dollars without a written request from the customer.
【中譯】根據更新後的門市政策，無論是週末輪班主管__還是兼職賣場員工，均無權在未獲得顧客書面申請的情況下批准超過五十美元的退款。
A
neither...nor
B
either...or
C
both...and
D
not...nor
解題關鍵
neither + A + nor + B = 否定並列結構，表示 A 和 B 兩者均不具備某資格或授權；為表達雙重否定的標準正式書面連接詞
陷阱提示
either...or（選擇其一）vs. neither...nor（兩者均否）：語意方向相反；both...and 肯定語境不接 nor；not...nor 為非標準語法
文法補充
neither...nor 辨析：① 語意：Neither A nor B = 兩者均不（雙重否定）② 對比：Either A or B（選一）/ Both A and B（兩者均）/ Neither A nor B（兩者均不）③ 動詞一致性：Neither A nor B + V → 動詞與 B 就近一致（Neither the manager nor the staff is...）④ 常見書面語境：強調「兩個本可能的例外均不適用」的政策聲明
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
neither...norconj./ˈnaɪðər...nɔːr/兩者均不（否定兩個並列項目的固定搭配）Neither A nor B = 「A 和 B 均不/均無」✓（雙重否定連接詞，強調兩者均不具備授權或資格）
either...orconj./ˈiːðər...ɔːr/兩者之一（表選擇，其中一方具備）Either A or B = 「A 或 B 其中之一」（表肯定的選擇性；語意相反於 neither...nor 的雙重否定）
both...andconj./boʊθ...ænd/兩者均（肯定並列）Both A and B = 「A 和 B 均…」（肯定語境；不接 nor；此句語境為否定授權，語意相反）

TOEIC · article
As artificial intelligence becomes increasingly embedded in corporate decision-making, many organizations are establishing AI governance committees to oversee the deployment and monitoring of AI systems. These internal bodies are tasked with setting standards for algorithmic accountability, reviewing high-risk use cases, and ensuring that AI outputs remain auditable by human reviewers.

However, critics argue that existing governance frameworks often lack teeth. In many cases, committee recommendations are non-binding, and business units are not required to implement suggested changes. Furthermore, transparency requirements vary widely: some companies publish detailed AI ethics reports, while others offer only vague statements that reveal little about actual practices. Industry analysts note that without enforceable policies and clear escalation procedures, AI governance committees risk becoming little more than a reputational shield for organizations.

Regulators in several jurisdictions are now moving to fill this gap. Draft legislation in the European Union and proposed guidelines from financial regulators in Asia-Pacific would require companies to document AI decision pathways, disclose training data sources, and designate a named individual responsible for each AI system. Proponents argue that such mandatory disclosures would create genuine accountability and help restore public trust in automated systems.

The Rise of Co-Living: Structural Shifts in Urban Rental Markets

Co-living, a housing model in which residents share communal spaces such as kitchens, lounges, and coworking areas while maintaining private bedrooms, has expanded rapidly across major cities in recent years. Operators such as The Collective and Common Living have not only transformed underutilized commercial buildings into vibrant residential communities but have also attracted significant institutional investment. According to a 2025 industry report, the global co-living market is projected to reach USD 13.9 billion by 2030, with Asia-Pacific cities accounting for the largest share of growth.

Despite its popularity among young professionals and digital nomads, co-living faces mounting criticism from housing advocates. They contend that co-living developments prioritize premium-priced amenities over affordability, effectively displacing lower-income residents from city centers. A recent survey conducted in three major metropolitan areas found that co-living units command rental premiums of 25 to 40 percent above conventional studio apartments of equivalent floor area. Critics have described this trend as a form of "luxury fragmentation" — dividing traditionally affordable housing stock into marketable experiential products accessible only to higher earners.

Property analysts, however, argue that the long-term structural impact of co-living remains ambiguous. Some researchers point to its role in activating vacant commercial properties and reducing urban blight, while others warn that without regulatory frameworks governing rent controls and occupancy standards, the sector may exacerbate existing housing inequalities. The debate ultimately reflects a broader tension in urban planning: how to balance market-driven innovation with equitable access to housing for all income groups.
