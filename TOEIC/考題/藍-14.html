Q1
Internal auditors discovered that the financial officer had been conducting ＿＿ transactions to funnel company funds into a personal account over a period of eighteen months.
【中譯】內部審計人員發現，該財務主管在十八個月內一直進行__交易，將公司資金轉入個人帳戶。
A
illicit
B
clandestine
C
fraudulent
D
speculative
解題關鍵
clandestine = 秘密進行/刻意隱瞞（強調隱密性）；illicit 泛指違法（無隱密性的強調）；fraudulent 已用 R158 Tier3 不可重複；speculative 偏投機性語意
陷阱提示
illicit 與 clandestine 最易混淆：illicit = 違法/不合規（重點在違規性）；clandestine = 秘密/暗中進行（重點在刻意隱瞞行為）；此題關鍵線索 discovered（被發現）+ funnel（暗中轉移）
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
illicitadj/ɪˈlɪsɪt/非法的；不合規的（泛指違法或違規行為）illicit transactions（正確語義，但較通用；偏強調「違法性」而非「隱密性」，未完全捕捉主動隱藏意圖的語氣）
clandestineadj/klænˈdɛstɪn/秘密進行的；暗中從事的（強調刻意隱瞞）clandestine transactions ✓（強調秘密/暗中進行；與 to funnel（暗中轉移）及 discovered（被發現）高度呼應；精準描述刻意隱藏的財務操作）
fraudulentadj/ˈfrɔːdjʊlənt/欺詐性的；詐騙性的（已用 R158，Tier3 不重複）fraudulent（已出現於 R158，Tier3 詞彙不可重複）
speculativeadj/ˈspɛkjʊlətɪv/投機性的；推測性的（偏財務風險推測）speculative investments（語意偏「高風險投資」，不含「秘密隱瞞」的意涵；與語境不符）
字詞家族
clandestine
adj. 秘密的；暗中進行的
clandestinely
adv. 秘密地；暗中地

Q3

【中譯】我們的客戶服務團隊加班工作，以便在季末前處理所有待處理的投訴。

Q7
The property developer's marketing campaign was praised for presenting the project in a highly ＿＿ way that resonated with first-time buyers.
【中譯】該房地產開發商的行銷活動因以高度__的方式呈現專案而廣受好評，引發了首次購屋者的共鳴。
A

B
related
C
relatable
D
relation
解題關鍵
highly + 形容詞 → 空格為形容詞；relatable = 易引起共鳴的（行銷語境常用詞）；related 語意不符（偏「相關的」，非「引發共鳴」）；relate 為動詞；relation 為名詞
陷阱提示
related 與 relatable 均為 relate 的衍生詞，但語意截然不同：related = 有關聯的；relatable = 能引起共鳴的
文法補充
Word Form 解題步驟：① highly + 空格 → 副詞修飾形容詞 ② relate 字根：relate（v.）/ related（adj. 相關的）/ relatable（adj. 易引起共鳴的）/ relation（n.）③ 語境線索：resonated with first-time buyers → 需表達「引發共鳴」的形容詞 → relatable
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
relatev/rɪˈleɪt/敘述；相關（動詞原形）relate to sb（動詞；此空格前有副詞 highly → 需形容詞）
relatedadj/rɪˈleɪtɪd/相關的；有關的（形容詞）a related issue（語意為「相關的」，不含「易引起共鳴」之意；與後文 resonated 語境不吻合）
relatableadj/rɪˈleɪtəbl/易引起共鳴的；貼近生活的（形容詞）a highly relatable way ✓（highly + adj. 副詞修飾形容詞；relatable = 讓目標受眾感同身受的；與 resonated 語境完美呼應）
relationn/rɪˈleɪʃən/關係；聯繫（名詞）a relation（名詞；不能跟在副詞 highly 之後作受詞，語法不符）

Q11
＿＿ that the budget had been finalized, the department heads proceeded to assign tasks to their teams.
【中譯】由於預算已確定，各部門主管開始為各自的團隊分派任務。
A
Having confirmed
B
Confirmed
C
To confirm
D
Confirming
解題關鍵
分詞構句中，完成式分詞 Having + pp 表示「先於主句動作完成的主動行為」；確認預算在前，分派任務在後，且主詞 department heads 主動確認，故選 Having confirmed。
陷阱提示
Confirmed 為過去分詞，表被動，主詞不是被確認的對象；Confirming 表同步，時間順序不符。需注意完成式分詞 Having + pp 與一般現在分詞 -ing 的差異。
文法補充
完成式分詞構句 Having + p.p. 表主動且先於主句：Having finished the report, she left the office.
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
Having confirmedv-ing(完成式分詞)/ˈhævɪŋ kənˈfɜːrmd/（已）確認後完成式分詞構句，表示主動且動作先於主句
Confirmedv-pp(過去分詞)/kənˈfɜːrmd/被確認過去分詞構句表被動，但主詞 department heads 是主動確認者，故不適用
To confirmto-inf/tə kənˈfɜːrm/為了確認不定詞表目的，但後句 proceeded 說明動作已在確認後發生，非目的關係
Confirmingv-ing(現在分詞)/kənˈfɜːrmɪŋ/正在確認現在分詞表同時進行或伴隨，但確認預算發生於分派任務之前，時間順序不符

Ocean Pollution and the Rise of the Circular Economy

Recent studies have documented an alarming increase in marine debris, with plastic waste now constituting more than eighty percent of ocean pollution. Coastal industries and consumer societies generate millions of tons of plastic annually, much of which enters ocean ecosystems through rivers, storm drains, and improper waste disposal.

In response to this crisis, a growing number of corporations and governments are adopting circular economy principles. Unlike the traditional linear model of produce-use-discard, a circular economy aims to eliminate waste by keeping materials in use for as long as possible. Products are designed for durability, repairability, and eventual recycling.

The impact of this transition has been so much so that certain coastal regions report measurable reductions in floating debris within just three years of implementing circular procurement policies. Marine biologists have noted improvements in biodiversity indicators near ports where single-use plastics have been banned.

However, critics caution that the circular economy alone cannot reverse decades of environmental damage. Large-scale ocean cleanup operations remain underfunded, and international agreements on marine pollution still lack enforceable mechanisms. Sustained investment and binding cross-border cooperation are considered essential if meaningful progress is to be achieved by 2035.
