B

C

D

解題關鍵
空格前有 a significant __，需要名詞；investment 為「投資」名詞，搭配 a significant investment in 正確。
陷阱提示
invest 為動詞；investing 雖可作動名詞但搭配不自然；investable 為形容詞。
文法補充
Word Form 解題步驟：1. 看空格前後詞性需求（冠詞 a + 形容詞 significant + __ = 名詞）；2. invest 字根四變形：invest(v)/investment(n)/investing(v-ing)/investable(adj)；3. 選名詞 investment。

Critics argued that the regulatory body's repeated ＿＿ in citing outdated legal frameworks undermined its authority among international compliance experts.
【中譯】批評者認為，監管機構反覆引用過時法律框架的__削弱了其在國際合規專家中的公信力。
A
malfeasance
B
negligence
C
fallacy
D
solecism
解題關鍵
solecism 源自希臘語，原指語法錯誤，延伸義指任何偏離規範或慣例的失當行為，在法律/學術語境中指引用不適當或違規的內容；fallacy 偏邏輯謬誤；negligence 偏疏忽；malfeasance 偏職務違法
陷阱提示
fallacy 最接近「錯誤」概念但偏邏輯推論；malfeasance 偏法律職務違法語意過重；solecism 精準指「引用失當/違反規範」
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
malfeasancen/mælˈfiːzəns/瀆職；不法行為（職務上的違法行為）malfeasance of office ✓（指職務瀆職，偏犯罪性質；此處強調知識錯誤，語意偏移）
negligencen/ˈnɛɡlɪdʒəns/疏忽；玩忽職守professional negligence ✓（偏疏於注意，不含知識/語言錯誤的語意）
fallacyn/ˈfæləsi/謬論；謬誤（邏輯/推論錯誤）a logical fallacy ✓（指邏輯推論錯誤；但不特指引用錯誤語境的專門術語）
solecismn/ˈsɒlɪsɪzəm/（語言/慣例上的）違規；失當行為a legal solecism ✓（指在正式/學術語境中引用了不適當或違反慣例的內容；最精準）
Q3
Neither the CEO nor the department heads ＿＿ informed of the proposed restructuring plan before the announcement.
【中譯】執行長及各部門主管均未在公告前__獲悉擬議的重組計畫。
A
were
B
was
C
have
D
had
解題關鍵
neither...nor 的主謂一致遵循「就近原則」：動詞依最靠近的主語決定；最近主語為 the department heads（複數）→ were；時態為過去式（before the announcement）→ were informed。
陷阱提示
就近原則（proximity rule）：neither A nor B → 動詞依 B 決定；B = heads（複數）→ were。若 B 為 the CEO（單數）則選 was。
文法補充
Neither A nor B + V：動詞依 B（最近主語）決定；同類結構：Either A or B、Not only A but also B
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
wereaux/wɜːr/過去式複數 be（就近原則）neither...nor → 動詞依最近的主語 ✓
wasaux/wɒz/過去式單數 bewas 對應單數最近主語 the CEO
haveaux/hæv/現在完成（複數）have been → 現在時態，不符
hadaux/hæd/過去完成助動詞had been → 過去完成，時態過重
Q4
By the time the revised quotation ＿＿ sent to the client, the original offer had already expired.
【中譯】在修訂後的報價被寄送給客戶__，原始報價早已過期。
A
was
B
is
C
has been
D
was being
解題關鍵
'By the time + 過去簡單式' 描述兩個過去動作同時進行中，從句動作以過去進行式被動（was being sent）表達「正在被送出」，主句以過去完成式（had already expired）表達「已先完成」。
陷阱提示
此句易誤選 was sent（過去簡單式被動），但 by the time 引導的時間子句強調的是動作「正在進行」的背景，故須用 was being sent。
文法補充
過去進行式被動 = was/were being + pp；與過去完成式（had + pp）搭配，表示兩個過去動作的先後關係。
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
wasaux/wɒz/過去式 be（用於被動）主動：S + was + Ving；被動：S + was + pp
isaux/ɪz/現在式 be（用於被動）現在簡單式被動：is + pp
has beenaux/hæz biːn/現在完成式被動has been + pp（強調到現在的結果）
was beingaux/wɒz ˈbiːiŋ/過去進行式被動was being + pp（強調當時持續進行的動作）✓

Q10
The new overtime policy, ＿＿ was introduced last month, has been welcomed by most employees.
【中譯】上個月推出的新加班政策，__受到大多數員工的歡迎。
A
that
B
what
C

D
which
解題關鍵
先行詞 'The new overtime policy' 為物，且子句前有逗號（非限定性關係子句），必須用 which，不可用 that。
陷阱提示
that 不可用在前後有逗號的非限定性關係子句中；what 引導名詞子句而非形容詞子句。
文法補充
非限定性關係子句（non-defining relative clause）用逗號隔開，引導詞必須用 which（物）或 who（人），不可用 that。
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
thatrel. pron./ðæt/關係代名詞 thatthat 不可用於非限定性（逗號）關係子句
whatrel. pron./wɒt/關係代名詞 whatwhat 引導名詞子句，不引導形容詞子句

whichrel. pron./wɪtʃ/關係代名詞 whichwhich 可用於非限定性關係子句（先行詞為物）✓

TOEIC · article
The Hidden Cost of Generosity: How Return Policies Shape Brand Loyalty

For years, e-commerce retailers have treated lenient return policies as a straightforward competitive tool — the assumption being that the easier it is for customers to return unwanted items, the more willing they will be to make a purchase in the first place. Recent research, however, suggests that the relationship between return policies and brand loyalty is considerably more nuanced than this conventional wisdom implies.

A study conducted by the Retail Analytics Institute surveyed 4,200 online shoppers across five countries and found that while hassle-free returns did increase initial purchase conversion rates by an average of 18 percent, they also correlated with a 12 percent decline in repeat purchase rates over a 12-month period. Researchers attributed this counterintuitive finding to what they termed the "return comfort trap": customers who never experienced friction in the purchase process developed weaker emotional attachments to the brands from which they bought.

In contrast, brands that implemented moderately structured return processes — requiring customers to provide a brief reason for returns and offering store credit as the default option rather than a full cash refund — reported stronger customer retention figures. Respondents in this group were also significantly more likely to recommend the brand to others, suggesting that a degree of effort in the post-purchase experience may reinforce perceived product value.

Retailers, the authors caution, should not interpret these findings as a license to make returns deliberately difficult. Rather, the data supports a middle path: policies transparent enough to instill purchase confidence, yet structured enough to foster a sense of brand relationship that pure frictionless transactions cannot.
